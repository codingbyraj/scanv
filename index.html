Editorial Services

By our professional editorial services, we make sure that your English-language manuscript will be polished and ready for submission to your publication of choice at a very reasonable price.

Our editorial services consist of the following:

Copy Editing

  • Manuscript styling in different styles i.e. American Psychological Association (APA), Chicago Manual, etc.
  • We have system of two reviews; your manuscript is reviewed by different editors to ensure quality editing.
  • Your manuscript is edited according to your style guide and specifications.
  • Editing is done by experienced professionals.

Proof Reading

  • Text Matching
  • Checking spelling and grammatical errors
  • Check formatting such as capitalization, font size, font style etc.
  • Check for captions, pagination
  • Check whether images are correctly placed and captioned.

Indexing

We have an in-house team of qualified indexers and a group of freelancers for all types of book and journal indexing.
